**dimension.sh** has now been shut down, thank you for 5 years of fun.

**dimension.sh** was a small public linux shell host (or pubnix system) that is open to anyone who wants to learn, experiment, and socialize with other like minded people. We offer a small collection of services, and hosting for web, gopher, and gemini sites.
